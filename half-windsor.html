HOW TO TIE A HALF WINDSOR KNOT

The Half Windsor Knot

The half windsor is an extremely versatile knot. It is actually three quarters the size of the windsor knot. It is of medium size, pretty symmetrical, and when tied correct - it creates a dimple within the knot. this knot is best used with skinny to medium sized ties.

  1. Start with the wide end of the tie on the right and the small end on the left. The tip of the small end should rest slightly above your belly-button. Only move the wide end.
  2. Wide end over the small end to the left.
  3. Under the small end and to the right.
  4. Up to the center, towards neck loop.
  5. Through the neck loop and to the left.
  6. Across the front over to the right.

  9. Tighten the knot by pulling down on the wide end. Slide the knot up and adjust.
